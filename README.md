## About Me

I am a Senior Unity Developer with **15+ years in the game industry**.

My mindset is strongly **system-oriented**. I do not just think about isolated features. I think about how gameplay, architecture, tools, pipelines, and production constraints fit together into one product that can actually ship.

I know how to **build a game from zero to product**:

- shape the technical foundation early
- build systems that can survive iteration
- align engineering work with product and client goals
- carry the work through delivery instead of stopping at prototypes

I am currently working as a **Client Lead at GearGames**, combining hands-on Unity engineering with product ownership, technical direction, and client-facing communication.

## Current Focus

Right now I am spending serious time **learning AI hard** and using it to improve how I work.

My main public focus is [**oh-my-skills**](https://github.com/cuozg/oh-my-skills), where I am building and refining reusable AI skills, workflows, and standards that help improve:

- engineering quality
- delivery speed
- development consistency
- practical day-to-day productivity

I am especially interested in using AI to make real production work better, not just to generate code faster.

## What I Bring

- **Systems thinking** for gameplay, architecture, tooling, and production
- **Zero-to-product execution** across the life of a game project
- **Unity engineering depth** with C#, editor tooling, architecture patterns, and workflow optimization
- **Client leadership** with clear communication grounded in technical reality
- **AI-assisted workflows** that raise team efficiency without lowering standards
